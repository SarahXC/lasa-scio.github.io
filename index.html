Science Olympiad

Welcome to the website of the Science Olympiad Club at the Liberal Arts and Science Academy (LASA) in Austin, Texas! Established in 2005, we have finished in the top 3 at State every year, and placed as high as 7th nationally. Thanks for your interest, and if you're a current or prospective LASA student, we hope to see you around!

If you have any questions or concerns, you can email us at lasa.scioly@gmail.com.

Science Olympiad


Science Olympiad is a national non-profit organization dedicated to improving the quality of K-12 science education, increasing male, female and minority interest in science, creating a technologically-literate workforce and providing recognition for outstanding achievement by both students and teachers. These goals are achieved by participating in Science Olympiad tournaments and non-competitive events, incorporating Science Olympiad into classroom curriculum and attending teacher training institutes.

Donate


Every year we travel to competitions in and out of Texas, and to prepare for these competitions we build devices and study textbooks as appropriate per event. In order to raise money for the travel, hotel, and material expenses, we hold a fundraiser every year in December where we give out free mistletoe and ask for small donations. If you'd like to come out, we appreciate your support! You're also able to donate here!
